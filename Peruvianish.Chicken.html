Peruvianish Chicken

the best Peruvianish Chicken

Want to wake up and not worry about making breakfast, well this recipe is for you. at the end of the day take 5 min to assemble these over night oats

Ingredients

    Marinade

  • 4-6 bone-in, skin on chicken thighs
  • 1 1/2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1/3 c soy sauce
  • 2 tsp agave
  • 2 1/2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 1/2 tsp lime zest
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 1/2 tbsp neutral oil
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  1. In a bowl, whisk all ingredients for marinade until well combined.
  2. Place chicken in a sealable bag & pour in marinade.
  3. Shake to coat. Marinate for 2-24 hrs, turning the bag occasionally.
  4. Preheat air fryer to 385°F & cook chicken for 8 mins per side or until crisp / fully cooked (thickest part reaches 165°F).
  5. you can also put it in the oven on 400°F for 25-30 minutes
  6. DONE
