High protein buffalo chiken quesadilla

the best High protein buffalo chiken quesadilla

Want to wake up and not worry about making breakfast, well this recipe is for you. at the end of the day take 5 min to assemble these over night oats

Ingredients

  • 1 cup shredded rotisserie chicken
  • 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 2-3 teaspoons hot sauce
  • salt, pepper and garlic powder to taste ~ two 8-inch tortillas
  • 1/3 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 tablespoon olive oil
  1. In a bowl mix together chicken, Greek yogurt, hot sauce and spices
  2. Sprinkle half the cheese on one of the tortillas, then an even layer of the chicken mixture
  3. Sprinkle on remainder of cheese then top with other tortilla
  4. Heat oil to medium in a nonstick pan, then cook quesadilla for 3-4 minutes per side, or until browned and crispy
  5. Cut into 4 slices and enjoy!
  6. DONE
