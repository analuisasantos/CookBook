Yugert Parfait

the best Yugert Parfait

just a few simple steps and youll have yourself a on the go breakfast sandwich

Ingredients

  • Yugert
  • PBfit
  • Banana
  • straberrys
  • bowl
  • granola
  1. In the bowl place yugert and PBfit
  2. mix well
  3. cut banana in to small slices and straberrys in halves
  4. throw the fruits in and add some granola on top
  5. DONE
