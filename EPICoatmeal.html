Epic Oatmeal

the best pic overnight Oatmeal

Want to wake up and not worry about making breakfast, well this recipe is for you. at the end of the day take 5 min to assemble these over night oats

Ingredients

    Base

  • 1/2 cup of old fashion rolled Oat
  • 1 scoop of protein (your choice of flaver)
  • 2/3 cup of milk of choice *i like almond milk*
  • 2 tbs plain greek yogurt
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1/4 tspvanilla extract

    Chocolate Chip

  • just add some Chocolate flavered protien powder
  • 2 tbs Chocolate chips
  1. Add each "base" ingredient, expect for the protein powder, to a mason jar (or any airtight container).
  2. Choose the flavor of overnight oats you plan to make. Add the corresponding flavor of protein powder and add-in to the jar. Stir, or place a lid on the container and shake to mix well.
  3. Put the container in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours, ideally overnight, to allow the oats to soak and soften before eating.
  4. DONE
