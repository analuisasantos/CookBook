Breakfast sandwich

the best breakfast sandwich

just a few simple steps and youll have yourself a on the go breakfast sandwich

Ingredients

  • two slice of bread (your choice)
  • egg
  • tomato
  • lettuce
  • ham,turkey, or any meat of your choice
  1. cut up the tomato
  2. place your bread in the toster
  3. scramble egge on a pan on medium heat
  4. when eggs start to get less runny place choice of meat on top and let it sit for 1 min
  5. then flip and lieave for 50 seconds
  6. place egg on on the the pieces of bread
  7. place tomato and lettuce
  8. DONE!

Enjoy
