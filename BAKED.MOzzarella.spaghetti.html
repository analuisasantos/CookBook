Air fried chiken wings

best Air fried chiken wings

Ingredients

  • 1 tbs Butter
  • 1 tbs Plain Flour
  • 1/4 cup Milk
  • 1 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 /2 tbsp (optional)Cajun seasoning
  • 1 tsp ( optional)Chilli flakes
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp Dried Basil
  • 1/2cup Cream
  • 1/4 cup Pasta water
  • Cooked Spaghetti
  • 1/2 Cup Mozzarella (shredded)
  1. In a pan add butter and flour, keep stirring and cook for atleast 1 minute.
  2. Now switch off the heat and add milk and keep stirring to make lump free roux.
  3. Once your roux is smooth, switch on the heat and cook for about few seconds and add seasonings and cream.
  4. Mix well and add cheese and pasta water.
  5. once cheese melts add Cooked Spaghetti and mix till the pasta is coated in cheese.
  6. Transfer the pasta into a baking dish and bake for about 15 minutes or until the top is golden and
  7. done
